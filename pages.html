About Us

Our History

"Joud Association is a community organization founded in 2010. Its original idea was to teach children about religion, starting with Quran memorization. In the beginning, there was no official center, and classes were held in the mosque, with a small fee for participation. Later, Mrs. Sanae Al Idrissi, who became the president of the association, decided to cover all the costs and establish a proper center near Al Joud Mosque. This is how the association got its name. Today, Joud Association serves both children and women, offering lessons in Quran, Islamic studies, and more, to help them learn and grow in their faith."
