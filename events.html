Our Events

Khatm Quran

Celebrating the Quranic Journey

At Joud Association, we are proud to organize special events to celebrate those who have completed the full recitation of the Quran, known as Khatm Quran. These events are not only a moment of celebration but also a way to honor the dedication, patience, and effort required to reach this significant spiritual milestone. The Khatm Quran events are held in a warm and welcoming environment, where participants are recognized for their hard work. During these events, we hold prayer sessions, Quranic recitations, and speeches to inspire and encourage both the participants and the audience. The atmosphere is filled with joy and reverence, creating a sense of unity among the community members.

Completing the Quran Together

These celebrations serve as an example to others, especially to the younger generation, to motivate them to continue their Quranic journey. We believe that completing the Quran is not only a personal achievement but also a collective inspiration to strengthen our faith. By showcasing the achievements of those who have completed their recitation, we hope to encourage others to take on the challenge and stay committed to their Quran studies. Our events also provide a platform for everyone to connect, share their experiences, and receive the support they need on their own Quranic path. Through these gatherings, Joud Association aims to foster a deeper understanding of the Quran, inspire lifelong learning, and help the community grow spiritually.

Hajj & Umrah

Making Hajj & Umrah Accessible

At Joud Association, we understand the importance of Hajj and Umrah as significant spiritual journeys for Muslims. We believe that these sacred pilgrimages should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation. To support this, we organize special events and fundraising initiatives to help provide Hajj and Umrah tickets for those who cannot afford them. Our events are designed to raise awareness about the costs associated with these pilgrimages and to gather donations from the community. Through these efforts, we aim to assist individuals and families who wish to perform Hajj or Umrah but face financial barriers. By working together, we can make these spiritual journeys possible for more people.

Supporting Spiritual Journeys

In addition to the fundraising events, we also offer guidance and support to those preparing for Hajj and Umrah, ensuring they are spiritually and practically ready for these important pilgrimages. Our goal is to make the entire experience more accessible, from ticket distribution to providing resources for proper preparation. Through our Hajj and Umrah initiatives, Joud Association seeks to foster unity, generosity, and faith within the community. We are committed to helping individuals fulfill their religious obligations and strengthen their connection to their faith by making these pilgrimages more attainable for those in need.

Together We Can Help
Ramadan Food Baskets

During Ramadan, Joud Association provides food baskets to support families, ensuring they can fast comfortably and have enough nourishment.

School Supplies Assistance

Joud Association helps families in need by providing essential school supplies to ensure every child has the tools needed for success in school.

Eid al-Adha Sacrifice Assistance

During Eid al-Adha, we raise funds to help families perform the sacrifice, making this religious practice accessible to those in need.
