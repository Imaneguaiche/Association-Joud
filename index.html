Pillars of Islam

Shahada

The declaration of faith, professing that there is only one God and Muhammad is His prophet.

Salat

The ritual prayer performed five times a day, facing the Kaaba in Mecca.

Zakat

The giving of a fixed portion of one's wealth to the needy, a form of almsgiving.

Sawm

Fasting during the month of Ramadan, abstaining from food, drink, and other specific activities from dawn to sunset.

Hajj

The pilgrimage to Mecca, which every Muslim must undertake at least once in their lifetime if they are able.
